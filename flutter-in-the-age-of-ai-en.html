01
DON'T JUST GENERATE — LEARN TO JUDGE THE CODE

84% of devs use AI, but only 3% "highly trust" the output. The expensive skill = knowing what's wrong/nonsense/unsafe. Don't skip the fundamentals.

02
RAISE YOUR ALTITUDE

For every feature ask: "for whom? what problem? how do we measure success?" — the muscle used in the loop above.

03
USE AI TO GET SMART FASTER — NOT TO STOP THINKING

For every AI answer, ask "why?" until you get it. The goal is to level up your own skill, not outsource your brain to the machine.
